Logitech G partners with many of the world's top esports athletes to design the PRO line with the goal of creating extreme performance gear that lets professionals play at the peak of their potential. The latest Logitech G PRO HERO Gaming Mouse has been updated with the new HERO 16K sensor and a low-friction, flexible cable. HERO 16K optical sensor is the highest performing and most efficient gaming sensor Logitech has ever made. An all-new lens and an updated tracking algorithm deliver ultra-precise tracking with no acceleration, smoothing, or filtering over the entire DPI range. Mechanical Switches and a Spring Button Tensioning system provide the near instantaneous button actuation that pro gamers demand. Logitech LIGHTSYNC technology lets you easily customize brilliant RGB lighting from approximately 16.8 million colors, while six programmable button can be setup with custom commands for your favorite games. Ultra-comfortable and durable, PRO HERO Gaming Mouse is built and tested to perform for an incredible 50 million clicks—the equivalent of a pro gamer training 10 hours a day for five years.
